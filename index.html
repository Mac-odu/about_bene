About Benedicta

Benedicta Tabuaa

In a bustling neighborhood where the sun's warmth embraced narrow alleys and colorful houses, there lived a young lady named Benedicta Tabuaa. She was a gentle presence, her laughter as melodious as the wind rustling through the leaves in the courtyard where we first met.

Benedicta and her mother had moved into the apartment close to ours a year ago. I still remember the day I first saw her, a petite figure with a radiant smile that could brighten even the cloudiest of days. Curious to know more about our new neighbors, I struck up a conversation with her one afternoon.

As we engaged in a coversation, she shared tales of her hometown, Dormaa, nestled in the heart of the Bono Region in Ghana. She spoke fondly of her days at Dormas Senior High School, where she had been a diligent student and a respected class captain. Her eyes sparkled with pride as she recounted her achievements and the friendships she cherished.

After completing high school, Benedicta worked at a local photo studio for a few years. Her days were filled with capturing memories for others, freezing moments of joy and solemnity in photographs that told stories beyond words. But her heart yearned for more—a calling she couldn't ignore.

With unwavering determination, Benedicta pursued her dream of becoming a nurse. She gained admission to study General Nursing at the tertiary level, diving headfirst into a world where compassion and skill intersected to heal and comfort those in need. Her decision to pursue nursing reflected her compassionate soul, a trait that shone brightly in every interaction.

Over the years of living in close proximity, our paths intertwined in unexpected ways. We shared stories over dinners and exchanged smiles in passing. Benedicta's visits to my workplace in Nkwabeng, which is not far from where they are currently living, has become a cherished routine. Her presence brings a sense of calm amidst the busyness of my days.

Time has passed, I've found myself drawn to you in ways that transcend mere friendship. Your kindness and quiet strength resonate deeply within me, and I've began to wonder about the possibility of something more. Yet, the uncertainty linger—is

are you single?
